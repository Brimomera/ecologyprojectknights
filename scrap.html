A Love Letter

My Dearest,

Your eyes, they are pretty. The way you smile is pretty. The way you laugh is pretty. YOU ARE PRETTY.... That's who you are. In the eyes of all, they are perfect, in mine, you are.

Everything you do has a reason and must have a reason. I don't like you, I admire you. I find you perfect, the perfect human, created by God just to display His power... I love your hair. I love you and I will keep on doing it until the day of judgment.

Oh, but do these words mean more than you?! Am I merely a poet or am I in love with you? I say I am, I don't want you to value my words, I want you to value me.

In your presence, time itself seems to stand still, a gentle whisper of eternity. My world, once a canvas of muted shades, now bursts with vibrant hues, painted by the light of your laughter. Every shared glance, every unspoken thought, weaves a tapestry of connection that binds our souls.

Here's something I wrote for you:
Baad e ishq munafiq hua ma
apki wajah se kafir hua me
log kehte hein mujh se shirk krta ha tu
apke madhab ka aalim hua ma
Izhar e muhabbat mushkil ha magar na mumkin nhi
mere lab band ha lekin annkhe bhut kuch kehti rhi
Mere dost mujhe chidhate hein
apke gesson ki qasmein khilwate hein
kehte ha jani waqt pani ki trh behta ha
tu akhir usse kuch kyu nhi kehta ha

Truly Yours,
Rauf
